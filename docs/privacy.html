Privacy Notice

This privacy notice discloses the privacy practices for the chrome extension "Granblue Integrated Raid Finder" (with ID: pebpijdcljjkhjiakkiiblmapcpgddln, referred to "this extension" in the following text) which acts as a auxiliary tool for the online game "Granblue Fantasy" (referred to "the game" in the following text). This privacy notice applies solely to information collected by this extension. It will notify you of the following:

  1. What personally identifiable information of you is handled, and how your information is used and with whom it may be shared.
  2. The security procedures in place to protect the misuse of your information.

Information Handling

Your following personally identifiable information are involved during using of this extension:

  1. Access token for the game, which is generated by the game after login and stored locally in your browser as a cookie.
  2. Your in-game status of the game, which includes how many consumable items you currently possesses, how many Action Points (aka. AP in the game), Battle Points (aka. BP in the game) you currently have.
  3. Contents of twitter pages viewed from your account (when you are logged in) or from your browser (when you are not logged in).

These information are used for the following purposes:

  1. Access token - for simulating user operations to fulfill convenient features, which includes automatically joining Raids/Co-op rooms and fetching Co-op room list.
  2. In-game status - for checking whether consumable items should be used in order to join Raids/Co-op rooms.
  3. Contents of twitter pages - fetching Raid Requests directly from twitter to fulfill the core feature of this extension.

We declares that these information are not collected by this extension, which is to say, we do not store them locally or remotely, we do not send these information over network to other websites or servers. So your information are not used solely by this extension and not shared with other party. We will not sell or rent this information to anyone.
